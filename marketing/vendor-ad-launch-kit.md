# SpontiCoupon — Vendor Acquisition Ad Launch Kit

The async funnel that replaces cold calling:
**HeyGen avatar ad (Orlando, $5–10/day) → Meta Lead Form → you build their DRAFT page → email them the live link → they say yes → flip live.**

Founder voice: Heriberto. Tone: human, casual, generous. Never say "sign up" to a vendor — say "I'll set it up for you."

## 2. META LEAD FORM (Instant Form)

Keep it SHORT — every extra field loses leads. Build in Ads Manager → the ad → "Instant form."

**Form type:** More volume

**Intro (headline):**
> Get Your Business on SpontiCoupon — Free

**Intro description:**
> We'll feature your business and promote your deals to Orlando locals — free for 3 months, no credit card. Drop your details and Heriberto will build your page and send you the live link this week.

**Questions (this order):**
1. Full name *(prefilled)*
2. Email *(prefilled)*
3. Business name *(short answer — custom question)*
4. What does your business do? *(multiple choice: Restaurant / Café / Bar · Spa / Salon / Barber · Gym / Fitness · Escape Room / Entertainment · Attraction / Tour · Auto · Retail / Shop · Other)*
5. Phone number *(prefilled — optional)*

**Privacy policy URL:** https://sponticoupon.com/privacy

**Completion screen:**
- Headline: *You're in! 🎉*
- Description: *Heriberto is building your free page now. You'll get an email with your live link within 1–2 days. Talk soon!*
- Button: **Visit SpontiCoupon** → https://sponticoupon.com
